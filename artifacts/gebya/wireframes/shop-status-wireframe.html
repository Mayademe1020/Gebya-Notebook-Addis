Design Principles

  • One story per screen — The owner opens this page to know "how's my business?" Answer in 2 seconds.
  • Progressive disclosure — Summary first, expand for details, link to full page for deep dive.
  • Adaptive sections — Empty sections hide. No empty cards. "All good" when nothing needs attention.
  • Natural language — "Top selling: Sugar, Oil, Bread" beats "Item: Sugar, Qty: 24, Revenue: 3,200".
  • Software does the thinking — Don't show 6 numbers and ask "was it good?" Compute profit and say "Yes, +2,400 ETB".
  • Information hierarchy — Story (30%) → Money (25%) → Activity (20%) → Attention (conditional) → Operations (collapsed) → Records (collapsed).
  • Warm, calm style — Paper notebook aesthetic. Green primary (#1B4332). Warm tones (#fafaf5, #ece6d6).

Module Specifications
