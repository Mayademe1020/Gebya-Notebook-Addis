The thinking behind it
Why this order:
  1. "How did yesterday go?" — The owner wants to know if things are on track before starting. One number, one sentence.
  2. "Who owes me?" — Credit is the biggest risk. Show it immediately so the owner can act.
  3. "What should I do?" — Translate data into actions. Not numbers — next steps.

Notice: no "Revenue" card. No "Expenses" card. No "Cash Flow" card. The owner doesn't think in those terms at 8:15 AM.

The thinking behind it
Closing ritual:
  1. Final tally — "Did I make money?" Answered immediately.
  2. Count cash — The most important action. Prominent button.
  3. Credit check — "Who's still owed?" Sets up tomorrow's follow-up.
  4. Diary entry — Auto-generated reflection. Scrollable history over time.

The diary is the key differentiator. No POS has this. It turns the app into a shop's memory.
