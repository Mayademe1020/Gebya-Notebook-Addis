What changed?

Accountant's approach (wrong)

  • Organizes by financial category: Revenue, Expenses, Cash, Credit
  • Shows 6 KPI numbers with no context
  • Requires the owner to interpret: "Was today good?"
  • Mixes earning and spending in separate cards
  • No narrative — just numbers

Shopkeeper's approach (right)

  • Organizes by questions the owner actually asks
  • Answers each question directly: "Yes, +2,400 ETB"
  • Tells a story: "Better than your average"
  • Groups related info: "Who owes me?" shows all credit in one place
  • Each section has a clear next action
