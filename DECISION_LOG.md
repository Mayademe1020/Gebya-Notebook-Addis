# Gebya Decision Log

## Format for each entry
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Decision**: What was decided
- **Reason**: Why
- **What was rejected**: What options were not chosen
- **Next check**: When to revisit

---

## Log

### 2026-06-10 — Initial setup
- **Decision**: Focus Gebya on offline-first shop memory assistant for Ethiopian small merchants.
- **Reason**: The field test wedge is shop memory, Dubie credit, staff sales visibility, quick notes, and owner trust — not full POS or ERP.
- **What was rejected**: Full POS, ERP, payroll, HR, inventory management, marketplace, credit scoring.

---

## Rules for adding entries
- Only log real decisions, not ideas.
- Keep each entry short.
- Always record what was rejected, not just what was chosen.
- Update after each major product decision or field-test session.
