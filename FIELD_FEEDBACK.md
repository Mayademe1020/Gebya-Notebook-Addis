# Gebya Field Feedback

## Shop owner/staff feedback captured so far

1. Many shops have support staff.
2. Staff sell items during the day.
3. Owner or sales head may only reconcile at the end of the day.
4. Owner wants visibility into what each staff recorded.
5. Staff should be able to record sales from their own mobile phone.
6. Owner may want notifications for each sale, high-value sales, or daily summary.
7. Reports should support search by item code, item name, amount, staff name, note, and date.
8. Owner may want to check what price an item was sold for months ago.
9. Staff feature should support daily reconciliation, not become full HR.
10. Need to keep the app simple enough for real shops.

## Product interpretation
The staff feature is not payroll or HR.
It is a shop trust and reconciliation workflow:
- Who sold what?
- How much did they record?
- What cash should be reconciled?
- What did the owner see?
- What needs follow-up?
